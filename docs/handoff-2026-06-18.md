- **Serial chain** — every stage `uv sync`es the shared NFS .venv; concurrent syncs
  corrupt it (`project_uv_sync_venv_race`). Do NOT parallelize across models.
- **Per-model `cleanup_path`** — pool6 (`/work3` storagepool 6) had only **~23 GiB
  free** at launch (77/100 used); four bf16 merges (~18 GiB) won't comfortably
  co-exist, and this exact set (gemma, Phi) already died once on a work3 quota
  overflow. Cleanup keeps **≤1 merged checkpoint** on disk at a time.
- **Llama-3.2-3B reuses** `models/lora/meta-llama_Llama-3.2-3B-Instruct-aligned`
  (verified complete: `adapter_model.safetensors` present). Asymmetry to note: three
  adapters are fresh from current code, one (3B) is from 06-14. Fine for a draft.
- **CATEGORY = simple_python** (headline FT metric, comparable to the CD
  simple_python column). Harder categories are a follow-up:
  `CATEGORY=multiple bash scripts/hpc/run_ft_aligned_cross_family.sh`.

**A `set -e` bug bit the first submit** (function ended on a `[ -z ] && {...}` that
returns 1 in the success case, aborting after one job). Fixed to `if/fi; return 0`;
the orphaned job 28690168 was `bkill`ed and the chain re-submitted clean. The 19
